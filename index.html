Information

Don't trust all faucets because some are scams.
Don't trust any advertising, even in legitimate faucets.

A bitcoin faucet is a reward system, in the form of a website or software app, that dispenses rewards in the form of a satoshi, which is worth a hundredth of a millionth BTC, for visitors to claim in exchange for completing a captcha or task as described by the website. There are also faucets that dispense alternative cryptocurrencies. (Bitcoin faucets on Wikipedia)
